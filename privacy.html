Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

At Efoyta Tech, we are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors. This policy explains how we handle your personal data to ensure your printing experience is safe and secure.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Details: Your name, email, and phone for order processing.
  • Project Assets: Logos and designs uploaded for printing.
  • Transaction Logs: Secure payment confirmations (we do not store credit cards).

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to fulfill your orders and coordinate delivery to your specified address.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption. Your design files are only accessible by our production team for the duration of the printing process.

Third-Party Policy

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We only share data with essential partners like delivery couriers.
